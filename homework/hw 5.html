Turtle info

Turtles are reptiles.
There are 360 living and recently extinct species of turtles, including land-dwelling tortoises and freshwater terrapins.

More turtle

- Turtles are ectotherms or "cold-blooded", and are generally opportunistic omnivores.
- The largest living species of turtle is the leatherback turtle, which can reach over 2.7 m (8 ft 10 in) in length and weigh over 500 kg (1,100 lb).
